Recently the club participated in a tree decorating competition. we are still awaiting judging.
During the NCL competition, the students are given various scenarios to work through that encompass 9 major subject matter areas to include open source intelligence, cryptography, password cracking, log analysis, network traffic analysis, forensics, scanning and reconnaissance, web application exploitation, and finally, enumeration and exploitation. Each area exposes students to real-world scenarios in each respective subject matter to give them hands-on experience in the most common realms of cyber security.
